Frequently Asked Questions

How do I suggest a new recipe?

Use the contact form above and select "Recipe Suggestion" as your subject. Include the recipe details, ingredients, and instructions.

Can I report a bug or issue?

Absolutely! Select "Bug Report" in the contact form and describe the issue you encountered. Include details about your device and browser.

Do you have a mobile app?

Recipe Finder is a web application that works great on mobile devices. No app installation required!

Is Recipe Finder free to use?

Yes! Recipe Finder is completely free to use. No registration or payment required.

How can I contribute to Recipe Finder?

We welcome contributions! Contact us with "Feature Request" to suggest improvements or new features.

Do you store my search history?

No, we don't store your search history or personal data. Your privacy is important to us. See our Privacy Policy for details.
